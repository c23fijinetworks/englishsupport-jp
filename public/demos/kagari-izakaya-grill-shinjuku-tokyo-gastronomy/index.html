Izakaya Etiquette

House Rules

01

Otoshi Service

A seasonal appetizer (Otoshi) is served to every guest upon seating. A table charge of ¥800 applies.

02

Seating Limits

To ensure all guests enjoy the Kagari experience, seating is limited to 2 hours during peak service.

03

Smoke-Free

We maintain a strictly non-smoking environment to preserve the delicate aromas of our charcoal grill.
